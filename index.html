Vivicheck is a smart software that assists in the diagnosis of the flu and cold. By inputting key medical information, Vivicheck can produce a diagnosis with a level of significance, sensitivity, and specificity. Vivicheck will rely on information recorded by a smart mobile device and a picture.

Donations will be used to produce the user interface and to develop methods for modern levels of sensitivity and specificity. Vivicheck will be one of the first in mobile diagnostics reducing the costs of healthcare for people. Vivicheck will connect people with a positive diagnosis to a pharmacist to obtain an appropriate medication to treat their illness. We hope to expand Vivichecks capabilities for several other diseases including bronchitis and calculating risk ratios for chronic disorders or lethal diseases.

FAQ

How much will Vivicheck cost?
Vivicheck will be a downloadable app costing $1.99 for a download in the play store and istore. There is no charge per check up.
How long will a Vivicheck up take?
Vivicheck ups take about 5 minutes in total.
Do I need a doctor to interpret results for me and obtain a prescription?
Vivicheck will provide the interpretations instantly. Results should be used to obtain an appropriate treatment.
When will Vivicheck launch?
We will be releasing a public version of the Vivicheck mobile app in Spring 2014.
